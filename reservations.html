Reserve your spot

Catnap


Join the adoptable kitties of Koneko for an hour of play -- or napping -- in our two-story cattery. Admission is $25 per person.

Whisker Wednesdays


Student? On a budget? We got you. We offer a discounted Catnap all day, every Wednesday. Bookings are $20/pp ($5 off/pp).

Kitty Brunch


Enjoy any food item and a beverage of your choice, plus Cattery admission, for $45! Thursdays and Fridays 11-1pm.

Thirsty Purrsdays


Happy hour! All catnaps receive one drink from 5 PM to close. Drinks can be alcoholic (beer, sake, etc.) or non-alcoholic (latte, orange juice, etc.).

Kids Hours


Two hourly slots for kids under the age of 8. All kids MUST have an adult chaperone with them during the visit. Please book for at least one adult with your reservation
CANCELLATION POLICY: PLEASE NOTE THAT RESERVATIONS CANCELED WITHIN 48 HOURS OF YOUR BOOKED TIME WILL NOT BE REFUNDED.
We uphold this policy strictly and do not make exceptions. Please plan your visit accordingly before booking.
If you wish to cancel your reservation and receive a refund, please email us with more than 48 hours notice at info@konekonyc.com.

We also require 48 hours notice to reschedule reservations. We cannot reschedule reservations with less than 48 hours notice.

You can reserve your spot in the Cattery using our online reservation system. You may book your time in hourly increments. We require payment at the time of booking -- $25 per person, per hour ($20/pp on Wednesdays).


Koneko's cattery is closed between 3 PM and 4 PM every day to feed our resident cats.


Groups of eight people or more will automatically have a 20% surcharge applied to their reservation. Reservations that are split in order to circumvent this surcharge (i.e. one reservation of 5 people, a separate reservation of 3 people) will be canceled at our discretion.


Children younger than 8 years old will not be allowed in the cattery outside of our Kids Hours, where all ages are welcome. Please do not attempt to book anyway if you have a child younger than 8 — we will cancel your reservation when you arrive and refund you.


Children between the ages of 8 and 14 must be accompanied by a parent or adult chaperone; Reservations must be made for the total size of your group (i.e. one parent and one child); we do not offer a discounted Catnap price for children.


If you are interested in having a private party at Koneko, please email us at info@konekonyc.com for details.
